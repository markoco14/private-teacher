The answers to some of your questions

What levels of English do you teach?
I teach all levels, from beginners learning the basics to advanced students preparing for professional settings or exams like IELTS and GEPT.
Do you offer online or in-person classes?
Currently, I offer online classes, allowing students from anywhere to join. If you’re interested in in-person lessons, feel free to contact me to discuss availability.
How long is each class?
Each class typically lasts 60 minutes. Longer or shorter sessions can be arranged based on your preferences and goals.
What materials do you provide?
I provide tailored lesson materials, including practice exercises, resources, and progress assessments. For coding classes, I may recommend tools and software to support learning.
What’s included in IELTS and GEPT preparation?
For IELTS and GEPT, I provide personalized lessons that focus on test strategies, time management, and skill-building in speaking, reading, writing, and listening.
How long will it take to see improvement?
The timeline for improvement depends on your starting level and learning goals. Many students notice significant progress within a few weeks of consistent lessons.
Are conversation classes suitable for shy learners?
Yes! I create a relaxed and supportive environment to help build confidence and fluency at your own pace.
Can absolute beginners join?
Absolutely! My beginner classes are designed to help you develop a strong foundation and make learning enjoyable.
Do I need prior experience for coding classes?
Not at all! My programming fundamentals class is perfect for beginners, while advanced topics like full-stack applications are suited for those with some coding experience.
What tools or software will I need for coding classes?
A laptop with an internet connection is sufficient. I’ll guide you on setting up any additional tools or software as needed during the lessons.
How can I book a class?
You can contact me directly through the Contact Me section or use the booking form on the website to schedule your first lesson.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or credit card. Please reach out for more details.
Do you offer refunds or rescheduling?
I offer flexible scheduling, and classes can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours' notice. Refund policies depend on the package or booking type—please contact me for details.
